Chicken and Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, chopped
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
  2. Combine 2 cups flour, baking powder, and 1 teaspoon salt in a bowl; cut in 1/2 cup butter with a pastry cutter or 2 knives until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in milk until dough holds together.
  3. Turn dough onto a floured surface and knead 10 times. Roll dough out to 1/2-inch thickness and cut into 2-inch squares.
  4. Bring chicken broth to a boil in a saucepan; reduce heat to low. Whisk 1/2 cup flour into 1/4 cup butter in a saucepan until smooth; gradually whisk in 2 cups chicken broth. Cook and stir mixture until thickened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Stir chicken, mixed vegetables, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and pepper into broth mixture; cook until heated through, about 5 minutes.
  6. Transfer chicken mixture to a baking dish and top with dough squares.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until biscuits are golden brown, about 10 minutes.
