Macaroni Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 3 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, chopped
  • 1 small red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 2 tablespoons dill pickle relish
  • 2 cups creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whipâ„¢)
  • 3 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup white vinegar
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon celery seed
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add the macaroni, and cook until tender, about 8 minutes. Rinse under cold water and drain.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the eggs, onion, celery, red pepper, and relish. In a small bowl, stir together the salad dressing, mustard, white sugar, vinegar, salt, celery seed, pepper and paprika. Pour over the vegetables, and stir until well blended. Refrigerate until chilled before serving.
